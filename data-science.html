Data Science¶

This course will enable technical teams by providing a comprehensive training that produces an efficient junior data scientist. Employees will build from the programming and mathematical fundamentals into the state-of-the-art. Students will journey through the landscape of machine learning spending time on supervised, unsupervised and deep-learning families of algorithms. High-performance computing and model deployment using containerization help ensure that the model-building is rooted in practical application.

Pre-requisites

  • math-stats-essentials

  • data-analytics
